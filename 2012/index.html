NIMHANS Convention Centre Bangalore 19 & 20 October

About

JSFoo is India’s only national JavaScript conference series, with previous editions in Bangalore, Pune and Chennai. This year’s Bangalore edition is a flagship two-day event.

The theme for JSFoo 2012 is JavaScript Everywhere. Hear from developers on how they and their organizations are pushing the limits of what can be done with JavaScript on the server, in embedded devices, and in its original home in the browser.

About HasGeek

HasGeek creates discussion spaces for geeks. Come to our events to meet like-minded people, learn new things and discover opportunities. HasGeek events are community-driven. Your money makes the event possible and your opinion determines how the event shapes up. HasGeek consists of a small core team of geeks holed up in a charming bungalow in Domlur, Bangalore, plotting awesome things, and a vast extended community of freelancers, volunteers, speakers and participants that includes you.
